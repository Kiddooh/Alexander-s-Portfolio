About Me

Front-End Developer | Tech Innovator | Digital Creator - Nairobi, Kenya

I am a passionate, versatile, and forward-thinking developer with a strong foundation in web development, digital marketing, and technical repairs. My tech journey started with simple computer study and grew into a full-scale passion that now spans Front-End Development, WordPress and Wix design, blockchain exploration, IPTV technology, and creative digital solutions.

Over the years, I've built, designed, and managed websites for businesses-including my own IPTV brands and expanded into areas like SEO, graphic design, and hardware repair. This diversity gives me a rare advantage: I understand technology from both the software and hardware side. Whether I'm building a modern UI, customizing WordPress, optimizing online presence for brands, or repairing a device, I bring precision and creativity to every task.

My experience also includes running and leading digital communities like TechTorch, building entrepreneurial projects, creating content strategies, and exploring advanced fields like AI, machine learning, and blockchain development. These interests shape my approach-always learning, always adapting, always innovating.

When I'm not coding at night (my favorite time to work), you'll find me exploring new technologies, networking with other professionals, studying AI/ML, learning chess tactics, creating business ideas, or enjoying music, travel, and good conversations.

I'm driven by impact-creating solutions that are clean, fast, modern, and meaningful.
